Tourist Places in Uttrakhand

1. Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a city and a municipal corporation in Dehradun district in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It is located in the foothills of the Himalayas on the banks of the Ganges River, 25 kilometres north of the state capital, Dehradun. Rishikesh is surrounded by forested hills and mountains on three sides and is known as the "Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas". It is also known as the "Yoga Capital of the World" and "River Rafting Capital of the World".
